# Diagora AI

> Where Medical Minds Meet

---

##  Overview

Diagora AI is a multi-agent clinical intelligence system that simulates how real medical specialists collaborate, debate, and refine diagnoses.

Instead of relying on a single AI model, the system orchestrates multiple specialized agents that analyze patient data, challenge each other’s assumptions, and produce a transparent, risk-aware clinical report.

---

##  The Problem

Modern AI healthcare tools often:
- provide a single answer without explanation
- lack transparency
- do not reflect real clinical collaboration
- hide uncertainty

In real medicine, decisions are rarely made by one perspective.

---

##  Our Solution

Diagora AI introduces:

> **AI-powered clinical board simulation**

The system:
- analyzes patient symptoms, vitals, and inputs
- simulates structured debate between AI specialists
- identifies disagreements and uncertainty
- produces explainable, risk-aware outputs

---
